Privacy

What this site collects, and what it doesn't.

This site counts pageviews per page, the domain sending you here, and whether you're on mobile. Nothing else is collected or stored. No cookies. No IPs retained. No identifier of you is created or kept.

Analytics are powered by signals in counter mode — an open-source, privacy-first observability tool built in this workshop.
